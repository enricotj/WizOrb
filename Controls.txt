For each force exerted on the orb, an opposing force is exerted on the player.

PC:
WASD = move player
Space = jump
Arrow Keys / Mouse (plus left mouse button) = move orb
Right Mouse Button = toggle orb movement mode (free-drag or auto-center)
Hold Left Shift = focus mode


XBox Controller:
Left Stick = move player
Click Left Stick = jump
Right Stick = move orb
Left Bumper = toggle orb movement mode (free-drag or auto-center)
Hold Right Bumper = focus mode
